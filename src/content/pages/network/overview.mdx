## How coverage works in KC

The metro is a game of relative elevation and clean horizons. If you can see across the river valley or over your neighborhood canopy, your node suddenly starts pulling messages from miles away.

### Indoor deployments

Indoor nodes are not wasted effort. Wood-frame homes with decent windows let plenty of RF out. Treat indoor installs as scouting missions: prove the site matters, then weatherproof and elevate later.

### Prosumer gear

You do not need broadcast equipment. Heltecs, T-Beams, SenseCAP trackers, RAK WisBlocks, and the occasional DIY Pi gateway make up 95% of the network. Match the configuration from the quick start checklist and they all play nicely.
